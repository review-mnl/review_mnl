About review.mnl

Your guide to finding the best board exam review centers in Manila

What is review.mnl?

review.mnl is a web platform dedicated to helping Filipino students find, compare, and enroll in professional board exam review centers across Manila, Philippines. Whether you're preparing for the Civil Engineering board exam, the Bar, or the Medical boards, review.mnl connects you with trusted review centers — all in one place.

flag

Our Mission

Our mission is to make quality board exam preparation accessible and transparent for every Filipino student. We believe every aspiring professional deserves to find the right review center — one that fits their course, budget, and schedule. By providing honest testimonials, clear ratings, and easy enrollment, we empower students to make informed decisions and review centers to reach the students who need them most.
